Awards and Certifications

Academic Awards

Sentinel Excellence Award for Mathematics

For the Sentinel Awards, Woodbridge High School teachers select students for academic achievement, class participation, student citizenship, and perseverance. Sentinel Award winners represent the top 3% of academic achievement at Woodbridge High School. I was chosen by my math teacher to receive this award in May 2020.

NCWIT Aspirations for Computer Science: Honorable Mention

The National Center for Women & Information Technology is a nonprofit organization that works to increase the meaningful participation of girls and women in computing.

The NCWIT Award for Aspirations awards high school girls for their computing-related achievements and interests. Award recipients are selected based on their aptitude and aspirations in technology and computing, as demonstrated by their computing experience, computing-related activities, leadership experience, tenacity in the face of barriers to access, and plans for post-secondary education. I received this award in April 2019.

Certifications

CompTIA is a non-profit association that issues professional certificates for the information technology industry. In June 2018 I received certification from CompTIA for IT Fundamentals.

Passed certification test for the following skills:

  • Knowledge and application of features and functions of common operating systems and establishing network connectivity
  • Ability to identify common software applications and their purpose
  • Knowledge of security and web browsing best practices

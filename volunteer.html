ACES basketball camp

I worked with ACES behavioral instructors to teach fundamental basketball skills to teenagers with autism. I led teenagers with autism through various basketball drills and competitions.

What is ACES?

ACES is an organization that serves individuals and families impacted by autism or other special needs.
